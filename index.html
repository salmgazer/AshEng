Design Lab

Design is that creative activity which seeks to improve the world by discovering better solutions to persistent problems, and by inventing solutions to new ones. This course presents current process-oriented and object-oriented design techniques using a framework that situates design activity within both the modern condition of commerce and computing, and the larger historical context of technological and commercial development.
