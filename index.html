Prof Skip Ellis Clarence

Was the first African-American to receive a Ph.D. in Computer Science. Dr. Skip Ellis worked as a researcher and developer at Bell Telephone Laboratories, IBM and Xerox amongst others with special interests in groupware, coordination theory, object oriented systems, CSCW, office systems, databases, distributed systems, software engineering, world-wide-web (internetworking), systems design and modeling, workflow systems, and humane interfaces to computers.
